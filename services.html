Services

The work, in plain terms.

Strategic communications. Storytelling. Digital strategy. Measurable results. Built for small nonprofits.

Service 01

Messaging and strategic communications

Most small nonprofits do not have a message problem. They have a consistency problem.

  • A messaging framework built on the three things you want to be known for.
  • Language every staffer, board member, and volunteer can carry.
  • Fundraising included. The appeal and the thank-you are messages too.
Service 02

Storytelling and content creation

Know the story before you gather it. Testimonials collected without a plan produce footage, not stories.

  • A standard question set, so every interview supports your key messages.
  • Narratives mapped to the three things you want to be known for.
  • Outline first. Then welcome the unexpected discoveries.
Service 03

Social media strategy

Consistency beats volume. Small teams win by showing up on a rhythm they can keep.

  • A channel plan sized to your actual capacity.
  • Content calendars built around your stories and your dates.
  • Metrics that matter, reported in plain language.
Service 04

Practical AI for small teams

AI should write with you, not for you. Learning it now pays off every week after.

  • Draft with more than one model. No single model is perfect.
  • Prompt with specifics: audience, tone, length.
  • Check every fact. Then edit until it sounds like you.

The house style

Every deliverable follows the rules Tony coaches.

Reader first. Small words. Short sentences. Short paragraphs. Get to the point. Bullets guide the eye. Outline, draft, revise.

Who this is for

Built for teams where communications is one slice of a bigger job.

Most of Tony's clients are not communications professionals. They run programs and raise money. Communications is wedged in between. The engagement respects that: clear recommendations, plain language, and systems a small team can actually run.
